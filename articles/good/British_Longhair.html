The British Longhair is an average sized breed of longhaired cat, originating in Great Britain.

This breed is the longhaired version of British Shorthair. Much like its shorthaired variety, the British Longhair has a broad square head. It is known as the Britannica cat in European countries, but this breed is not recognized in the UK.

Colors[]

This breed comes in all colors, except for Colorpoint
